Watch Mashle: Magic and Muscles Hindi

Mashle: Magic and Muscles is a 2023 anime series that parodies Harry Potter and the Wizarding World.
The story follows Mash Burnedead, a boy who is born into a world of magic but has no magical ability.
Mash rejects magic and instead dedicates himself to rigorous training and muscle-building.
By his teenage years, he is far more powerful than any normal man.
Mash enrolls at Easton and aims to fool everyone into thinking he's top of the class. He trains his body every minute of every day all of his life.
